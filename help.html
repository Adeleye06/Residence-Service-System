Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most frequently asked questions about our database management system. If you don't find the answer to your question here, please contact support.

  • The system indicates that the resident is already logged in?
    You probably already logged in, the website should automatically redirect you to the dashboard page. If not, click here to go to the dashboard page.
  • The system indicates that the OTP has expired?
    Residents should generate a new OTP and input the new OTP code. It is important to note that OTPs expire within 2 minutes. Please have your email ready before you generate the OTP.
  • The system prompts residents to input the OTP but I havenâ€™t received one?
    Residents should click generate a new OTP and copy it from their email to the system for logging in. You can not generate a OTP if you are not a resident or a admin. Please contact the residence office if you need assistance for login.
  • The system indicates that the user is not recognized as a resident?
    Residents should ensure they are using their school email address and try logging in again. If the issue persists, your information may be not correctly being added to the system, you should contact the residence office for assistance.
